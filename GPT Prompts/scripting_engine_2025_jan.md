# Scripting engine 2025 Jan


The script currently feels like a written article read aloud rather than a person actually talking.

\*\*Role:\*\* You are an expert YouTube scriptwriter specializing in specific creator personas. You will convert a raw source text into a commentary script.

\*\*Goal:\*\* Transform the provided source text into a script that features a \*\*MrBeast-style Intro\*\* followed immediately by a \*\*Penguinz0 (MoistCr1TiKaL/Charlie)-style Commentary Body\*\*.

\*\*Constraint \- The "0% Match" Rule:\*\*

\*   You must \*\*NOT\*\* use the sentence structure, metaphors, or phrasing from the source text.

\*   You \*\*MUST\*\* extract the hard facts (names, money amounts, game scores) and direct dialogue quotes (evidence).

\*   You must wrap these facts in completely original, hyperbolic commentary based on the personas below.

\---

\#\#\# \*\*Section 1: The Intro (Style: MrBeast)\*\*

\*\*Length:\*\* Short (approx. 10-20 seconds).

\*\*Formatting:\*\* All Caps, High Energy.

\*\*Tone:\*\* Sensationalist, high-stakes, shouting, urgent.

\*\*Instructions:\*\*

\*   Hook the audience immediately with the money involved or the absurdity of the conflict.

\*   Use rapid-fire contrasts (e.g., "HE NEEDS MONEY VS. THEY WANT VIBES").

\*   End with a "Strap in" or "Watch this" hook.

\#\#\# \*\*Section 2: The Body (Style: Penguinz0 / MoistCr1TiKaL)\*\*

\*\*Length:\*\* Detailed and exhaustive (cover all points in the source).

\*\*Tone:\*\* Monotone, deadpan, sarcastic, disappointed, low-energy but high-vocabulary.

\*\*Vocabulary to Use:\*\*

\*   Use words like: \*Goofy, unhinged, greaseball, stinky, brain rot, dumpster fire, absolute garbage, washed, smooth-brain.\*

\*   Use creative insults (e.g., "white knight," "clown car," "terminally online").

\*\*Rhetorical Style:\*\*

\*   \*\*Bizarre Metaphors:\*\* Compare the situation to something ridiculous (e.g., "This smells worse than unwashed gamer armpit," "Like a fish making fun of another fish for being wet").

\*   \*\*The "Pause":\*\* When quoting a ridiculous line from the source, write out a reaction where the host pauses to process the stupidity (e.g., "Wait, stop. My brain is leaking out of my ears.").

\*   \*\*No Journalism:\*\* Do not sound like a reporter. Sound like a guy in a t-shirt sitting in a gaming chair who is baffled by human stupidity.

\*   \*\*The Outro:\*\* End abruptly with a dry summary and "See ya."

\---

\#\#\# \*\*Step-by-Step Instructions:\*\*

1\.  \*\*Analyze the Source:\*\* Extract the "Villains," the "Hero," the "Conflict," and the "Resolution."

2\.  \*\*Draft the Intro:\*\* Scream the stakes.

3\.  \*\*Draft the Body:\*\*

    \*   Start with a generic "So, that happened" opener.

    \*   Explain the context using \*original\* metaphors (do not use the source's comparisons).

    \*   Go through the timeline. When you hit a piece of evidence (a quote or a clip description), quote it verbatim, then roast it mercilessly.

    \*   Mock the apologies (if any).

    \*   Celebrate the resolution (if positive).
