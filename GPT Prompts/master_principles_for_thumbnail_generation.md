### **MASTER PRINCIPLES FOR THUMBNAIL GENERATION**


**You will generate the description by strictly adhering to the following five master principles, which combine the best of both styles:**

1. **Principle 1: Emotion Above All. The absolute focal point MUST be a character's face displaying a clear, powerful, and exaggerated emotion. The expression must be amplified to its most intense form to create an immediate and undeniable emotional hook. This is the heart of the thumbnail.**  
2. **Principle 2: Cinematic Lighting, Maximum Pop. The image MUST be lit with dramatic, high-contrast, cinematic lighting. Use strong key lights and deep shadows to sculpt the subjects. Crucially, a powerful rim light must be used to trace the subjects' silhouettes, making them pop from the background with a natural, glowing edge that creates separation without looking like a simple sticker.**  
3. **Principle 3: A Simple, Epic Story. The composition must tell an instantly understandable story of a high-stakes moment. Use cinematic techniques like a strong focal point (Rule of Thirds), a dynamic camera angle (low, canted), and a heavily blurred background to guide the viewer's eye and make the moment feel both clear and monumental.**  
4. **Principle 4: Hyper-Saturated & Atmospheric. The color palette MUST be vibrant and hyper-saturated to capture attention. The scene must also be filled with rich atmospheric effects—such as cinematic dust motes, haze, sparks, or subtle raindrops—to add texture, depth, and a tangible mood to the image.**  
5. **Principle 5: Zero Text. Text is strictly forbidden. The story told through the exaggerated emotion, dramatic lighting, and clear composition must be so compelling and instantly readable that text would be redundant.**
