#### **STRUCTURE REQUIREMENTS**


The script must use viral YouTube video structure. Stakes rise at every act break — never fall. The detective revelation structure governs how and why they rise.

**HOOK \[0:00–0:30\]** — governed entirely by the MrBeast Hook Principle above. Three lines. Same flat fast register throughout. The viewer's own experience is the first image. Named subject arrives in line 2\. Two specific forward-pull questions in line 3, the second of which extends beyond the specific subject.

**ACTS — rising stakes through escalating crime structure:**

Each act answers the primary question raised by the previous act and raises a bigger one following the detective structure.

Each act gets one short production note immediately below the act heading, before any speech.

**RE-ENGAGEMENT MICRO-HOOKS:**

Place three re-engagement hooks at approximately the 2-minute, 5-minute, and 7-minute marks. Each is a forward pull — a specific promise of something not yet seen that creates an unresolved question. Not summaries. Formatted as visual callouts. Not spoken.

**Test each re-engagement hook:** Does reading it make the viewer form a specific unresolved question? If they could feel satisfied without watching further, it's a summary. Rebuild.

**CRYSTALLISING LINE:**

One line that captures the entire argument as a shareable verdict. In the final third. The payoff of the detective case. Not an observation — a proven conclusion. Highlighted visually. Spoken. The one intentional exception to the spoken flow rules.

**TONAL VARIATION:**

The highest drop-off window is approximately minutes 3–7. The tonal variation in this window is the investigator's register — an entertaining section revealing itself as evidence of something worse. Not inserted as a structural device — it arrives naturally at the escalation point. Paired with a personality moment immediately before or after to provide contrast.

**CONCLUSION — THE PROFOUND ENDING:**

Governed by the Profound Ending Requirement above. Moves from specific to universal. Names the universal pattern. Connects back to an earlier breadcrumb. Final line carries full weight because everything required to understand it has been established. The viewer leaves with something they cannot un-know.

Do not end on "I genuinely don't know." Do not end on intellectual uncertainty. End on a specific image that reframes something about how the world works.
